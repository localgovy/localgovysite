Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of businesses is this for?

Any local business with regular customers — restaurants, retailers, salons, gyms, studios, service businesses, entertainment venues, and more.

Do I need my own app developer?

No. We build and maintain everything. You manage your content through a simple dashboard — no technical skills required.

Do you build the website too?

Yes. Your app and website are built together as one connected system so you're not managing two separate tools.

Can I send promotions and deals to customers?

Yes. You can publish promotions, seasonal offers, and loyalty rewards to your app and website, and send push notifications directly to customers who have your app.

How long does it take to launch?

Typically 2–4 weeks from start to launch, including app branding, website design, content setup, and App Store/Google Play submission.
