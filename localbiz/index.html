Restaurants, retailers, salons, gyms, studios, service businesses, entertainment venues — any local business with regular customers.

No. We build and maintain everything. You can manage all relevant content through a simple dashboard — no technical skills required.

Can I send promotions and deals to customers?

Yes. You can publish promotions, seasonal offers, and loyalty rewards directly to your app, and send push notifications to customers who have it installed.

Typically 2–4 weeks from start to launch, including app design, content setup, and App Store/Google Play submission. During this time, you will pay nothing.
