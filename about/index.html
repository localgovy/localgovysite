About

Digital infrastructure for every community

We build beautiful, fully managed mobile apps for the local businesses and institutions that bring communities together — across Canada and the US.

Our story

Local businesses, faith communities, and municipalities all face the same problem: too many disconnected tools, and no single place for their people to go.

LocalGovy is a Canadian company based in Toronto, Ontario, building the next frontier of affordable, beautifully designed digital infrastructure — so a small shop or a growing congregation can have an app as polished as any national brand's.

We handle the design, the build, the app store submissions, and the ongoing support. You stay focused on serving your customers and community.

Noah Khan
Founder · Toronto, Ontario
