About LocalGovy

Connecting communities across Canada with innovative municipal technology.

Our Story

LocalGovy originated in Massachusetts with a vision to transform how municipalities communicate with their residents. What began as an innovative idea has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving communities across Canada.

Today, LocalGovy is a fully Canadian company based in Aurora, Ontario, supporting implementation in all provinces. We're committed to helping towns and cities across Canada strengthen their connection with residents through modern, accessible technology.

Noah Khan

CEO & Founder

Noah Khan founded LocalGovy with a mission to make municipal services more accessible and engaging for all residents. His vision combines cutting-edge technology with a deep understanding of community needs.

Under Noah's leadership, LocalGovy has grown from its Massachusetts roots into a proud Canadian company, now serving municipalities across all provinces from our headquarters in Aurora, Ontario.
