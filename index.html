Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LocalGovy

How long does it take to launch?

Typically 2-4 weeks from start to launch. This includes app branding with your town's colors and logo, content setup, staff training, and App Store/Google Play submissions.

Do we need technical expertise to manage the app?

No technical skills required! Our easy-to-use content management system allows your staff to post news, update events, and manage the app without any coding or technical knowledge. We provide comprehensive training and ongoing support.

How does the AI assistant work?

The AI assistant is trained on your town's specific information including hours, services, policies, and FAQs. It provides instant, accurate answers to common resident questions 24/7, significantly reducing phone calls and emails to your staff.

Can we customize the app for our town?

Yes! The app is fully branded with your town's name, logo, colors, and identity. It appears as your own branded app in the App Store and Google Play.

What about data privacy and security?

We take security seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS, we follow industry-standard security practices, and no personal information is required for most features. We're privacy-first by design.

Can local businesses be featured in the directory?

Yes! The business directory is free for all local businesses. Optional sponsored listings are available for businesses that want enhanced visibility, providing additional revenue for your town.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive onboarding and training for your staff, along with ongoing technical support. Our team is available to help with any questions or issues that arise.
