One Mission. Every Institution.

Communities — whether a town, a church, a gurdwara, or a mosque — face the same fundamental challenge: their members are scattered across too many disconnected tools. Old websites. Group chats. Social posts. Separate donation links. Spreadsheets.

LocalGovy's mission is to replace that scattered experience with one connected digital home — purpose-built for each type of institution, powered by shared infrastructure.

Purpose-Built

Every product is designed around the specific needs of its institution — not repurposed generic software.

Shared Infrastructure

Apps, websites, notifications, events, forms, and admin tools — built once, adapted to every context.

Canadian-Focused

Built in Aurora, Ontario. Designed for Canadian communities and the institutions that serve them.
