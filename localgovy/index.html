Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to launch?

Typically 2–4 weeks, including branding, content setup, training, and App Store / Google Play submission.

Do we need technical expertise to manage it?

No. Staff manage everything through a simple dashboard, and we provide full training and ongoing support.

How does the virtual assistant work?

It's trained on your town's hours, services, and policies, and answers residents around the clock — reducing calls and emails to staff.

Can we customize the app for our town?

Yes — your name, logo, and colours throughout. It appears as your own branded app on the App Store and Google Play.
