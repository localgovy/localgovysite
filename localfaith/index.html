Frequently asked questions

Is every app the same?

No. A mosque app shouldn't feel like a church app — we implement your specific prayer/service times, traditions, language, giving categories, and events, not just a rebranded copy.

Is this a white-label template?

No. We build on a proven platform, but every community's app runs on its own real data and workflows — satisfying Apple's and Google's app store rules against duplicate, cosmetic-only apps.

Do you build the website too?

Yes. The Complete Platform includes a matching website connected to the same system.

Can we accept donations?

Yes — recurring giving, campaigns, and categories like seva, zakat, and tithes.
