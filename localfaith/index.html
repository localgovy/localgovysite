Frequently Asked Questions

Is LocalFaith only for churches?

No. LocalFaith is built for churches, mandirs, gurdwaras, mosques, synagogues, temples, and other faith-based organizations.

Is every app the same?

No. The infrastructure is shared, but the experience is customized. A mandir app should not feel like a church app. Each version is built around the institution's language, events, giving needs, traditions, and community.

Do we need to replace our current tools?

Not always. LocalFaith can connect with or organize around the tools you already use. Over time, we can help simplify your digital setup.

Do you build the website too?

Yes. The main LocalFaith platform includes a custom website redesign so your app and website work together as one connected system.

Can we send push notifications?

Yes. LocalFaith allows your organization to send important updates directly through the mobile app to your community's phones.

Can we accept donations?

Yes. LocalFaith supports donation and giving flows, including recurring giving, special funds, campaigns, and faith-specific giving categories like seva, zakat, dasvandh, and tithes.

Can we manage events?

Yes. You can promote events, collect RSVPs or registrations, send reminders, and keep your community updated.
