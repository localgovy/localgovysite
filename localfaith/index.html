Frequently asked questions

Is every app the same?

No. The infrastructure is shared, but the experience is customized. A mosque app shouldn't feel like a church app — each is built around the institution's language, events, and traditions.

Do you build the website too?

Yes. The Complete Platform includes a custom website so your app and website work together as one connected system.

Can we accept donations?

Yes — including recurring giving, campaigns, and faith-specific categories like seva, zakat, dasvandh, and tithes.

Can we send push notifications?

Yes. Send important updates directly through the mobile app to your community's phones.
